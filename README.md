# Insight2Impact (I2I Youth Lab)

## Data-Driven Youth Internship Program

Insight2Impact (I2I Youth Lab) is a high-impact STEM internship program where students use data analytics, programming, mapping, and research to solve real-world social challenges such as food insecurity, poverty, and homelessness.

## 🌍 What We Do

Students work with real datasets from government and nonprofit sources to:

* Analyze community-level challenges
* Build dashboards and maps
* Develop data-driven solutions
* Present findings to simulated policy and leadership audiences

## 🧠 Skills Students Learn

* Data Analysis (EDA, cleaning, statistical thinking)
* Programming (R, Python)
* Data Visualization (Tableau, Excel)
* GIS Mapping
* Research & Survey Methods

## 🎯 Program Outcomes

Students graduate with:

* A professional data portfolio
* Research reports and dashboards
* Public speaking experience
* Exposure to careers in data science, policy, and social research
